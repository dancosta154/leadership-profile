How to Work With Dan

Communication preferences, working style, and collaboration best practices

Communication Preferences

  • Best: Slack for quick questions and updates
  • Good: Zoom for discussions and collaboration
  • Last Resort: Email (I prefer real-time communication)
  • Response Time: I aim to respond within 2-4 hours during work hours
  • Async Work: I appreciate detailed context in written form for asynchronous decisions

My Working Style

  • Problem-Solving: I dig deep to understand root causes, not just symptoms
  • Decision-Making: Data-informed but willing to make judgment calls when needed
  • Collaboration: I value diverse perspectives, encourage healthy debate, and work hard to create psychological safety so everyone feels comfortable speaking up
  • Flexibility: I adapt my approach based on the team and situation

What to Expect From Me

  • Transparency: I'll be honest about challenges and limitations
  • Directness: I value clear, straightforward communication
  • Follow-Through: If I commit to something, I'll deliver or communicate early if issues arise
  • Growth Mindset: I prioritize growth and learning and value constructive feedback
  • Approachability: Even if I seem serious, I'm always open to questions and conversations

How You Can Help Me

  • Context: Give me background on problems and decisions
  • Clarity: Be specific about what you need and by when
  • Questions: Don't hesitate to ask clarifying questions
  • Feedback: Let me know what's working and what's not
  • Collaboration: Bring ideas and solutions, not just problems

Meeting Preferences

  • Agendas: I appreciate clear agendas sent in advance
  • Punctuality: I start and end on time
  • Preparation: I do my homework before meetings
  • Action Items: I like clear next steps and owners
  • Efficiency: If it can be an email or Slack thread, let's do that instead

My Values at Work

  • Simplicity: Making complex things simple and accessible
  • Excellence: Doing quality work that makes an impact
  • Growth: Continuous learning and development
  • Collaboration: Building strong, effective teams
  • Integrity: Doing the right thing, even when it's hard
